Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2026

Vauchi is a privacy-focused contact card app. This policy explains what data Vauchi handles and how it is protected.

Summary

Vauchi does not collect, store, or share your personal data. All contact information stays on your device, encrypted under keys only you control.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location tracking
  • No usage statistics
  • No crash reports sent to us
  • No account registration (no email, no phone number)

Data Stored On Your Device

All data is stored locally on your device and encrypted with keys derived from your identity. This includes:

  • Your contact card (name, fields you choose to add)
  • Contact cards received from others
  • Cryptographic keys for end-to-end encryption
  • Visibility and label settings

You can delete all local data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the in-app data reset.

Data Transmitted

When you sync contact updates, encrypted data is transmitted through a relay server. The relay operates on a zero-knowledge basis:

What the relay sees What the relay cannot see
Encrypted blobs (opaque bytes) Your name, fields, or card content
Connection timestamps and IP addresses Who your contacts are
Message sizes What you changed or when

Encrypted messages are stored on the relay temporarily (up to 30 days) until delivered, then deleted. The relay cannot decrypt them.

Contact Exchange

Contact exchange requires physical proximity. You scan a QR code in person. No data is sent to any server during the exchange itself. The camera is used only for QR scanning and no images are stored or transmitted.

Permissions

  • Camera — QR code scanning for contact exchange. Activated only when you open the scanner.
  • Internet — Syncing encrypted updates through the relay. All data is end-to-end encrypted before transmission.

Third Parties

Vauchi does not share data with any third party. There are no advertising SDKs, analytics services, or tracking libraries in the app.

Children

Vauchi does not knowingly collect data from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, there is no age-specific data processing.

Open Source

Vauchi is open source. You can inspect the code at gitlab.com/vauchi to verify these claims.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
